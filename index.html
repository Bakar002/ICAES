1st International Conference on Advancement in Engineering Sciences

University of the Punjab New Campus Institute of Electrical, Electronics and Computer Engineering, Lahore 28-29 October 2024

About the Event

The International Conference on Advancement in Engineering Sciences will be held on 28-29 October 2024 at the Institute of Electrical Electronics & Computer Engineering, University of the Punjab, under the umbrella of the Punjab Higher Education Commission. The conference encourages the submission of original research papers, case studies, and technical presentations that contribute to the advancement of knowledge in the engineering field. Additionally, it welcomes interdisciplinary studies and encourages collaborations across different engineering disciplines.

Original Research articles and Technical Reviews can be submitted for the conference through on-line submission system. All papers are peer reviewed prior to editorial decision on the submissions.

Focus of the conference includes but is not limited to:

  • Agricultural Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Current issues and challenges in Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronic Engineering
  • Energy Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Genetic Engineering
  • Industrial Engineering
  • Informational Technology
  • Manufacturing Engineering and Technology
  • Materials Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Mechatronics
  • Optical Engineering
  • Petroleum Engineering
  • Power Engineering
  • Polymer Engineering
  • Renewable Energy Technology
  • Process Engineering
  • Systems and Software Engineering
  • Technology for Big Data
  • Technology for Cloud Computing
  • Textile Engineering
  • Vehicle Engineering
